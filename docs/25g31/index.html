Initial condition for abm-aor simulation 1 is discussed in this note.

mpm

A matrix named as movement probability matrix (MPM) is used to define probable new position of agents, which has form as follow

$$\tag{1} {\rm MPM} = \left[ \begin{matrix} m_{11} & m_{12} & m_{13} \newline m_{21} & m_{22} & m_{23} \newline m_{31} & m_{32} & m_{33} \end{matrix} \right], $$

where directions represented by each matrix element are given in following table.

ElementDirectionCompass Term
$m_{11}$Upper-leftNorthwest (NW)
$m_{12}$UpNorth (N)
$m_{13}$Upper-rightNortheast (NE)
$m_{21}$LeftWest (W)
$m_{22}$Stay(No movement)
$m_{23}$RightEast (E)
$m_{31}$Lower-leftSouthwest (SW)
$m_{32}$DownSouth (S)
$m_{33}$Lower-rightSoutheast (SE)

Or following

$$\tag{2} {\rm MPM} = \left[ \begin{matrix} \nwarrow & \uparrow & \nearrow \newline \leftarrow & \circ & \rightarrow \newline \swarrow & \downarrow & \searrow \end{matrix} \right] $$

is representation of MPM in more visual way.
