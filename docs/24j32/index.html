Seek info about calisthenics and motivation to perform in.

A form of resistance training that uses the weight of your body along with gravity is known as calisthenics 1, which is a high-intensity workout performed at a moderate pace without much rest time 2. This activitiy requires nearly no equipment, improves functional strength, increase flexibility and mobility, builds lean muscle mass, and provides a full-body workout 3.
