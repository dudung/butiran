Initial condition for abm-aor simulation [^viridi_2025a] is discussed in this note. 


## mpm
A matrix named as movement probability matrix (MPM) is used to define probable new position of agents, which has form as follow

$$\tag{1}
{\rm MPM} = \left[
\begin{matrix}
m_{11} & m_{12} & m_{13} \newline
m_{21} & m_{22} & m_{23} \newline
m_{31} & m_{32} & m_{33}
\end{matrix}
\right],
$$

where directions represented by each matrix element are given in following table.

{{< style class="table-0" >}}
Element  | Direction   | Compass Term
:- | :- | :-
$m_{11}$ | Upper-left  | Northwest (NW)
$m_{12}$ | Up          | North (N)
$m_{13}$ | Upper-right | Northeast (NE)
$m_{21}$ | Left        | West (W)
$m_{22}$ | Stay        | (No movement)
$m_{23}$ | Right       | East (E)
$m_{31}$ | Lower-left  | Southwest (SW)
$m_{32}$ | Down        | South (S)
$m_{33}$ | Lower-right | Southeast (SE)
{{< /style >}}

Or following

$$\tag{2}
{\rm MPM} = \left[
\begin{matrix}
\nwarrow   & \uparrow   & \nearrow    \newline
\leftarrow & \circ      & \rightarrow \newline
\swarrow   & \downarrow & \searrow
\end{matrix}
\right]
$$

is representation of MPM in more visual way.
