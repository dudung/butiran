+ Position is the point or area occupied by a physical object [^merriam_webster_2025].
+ Position in physics means a unique location in space with reference to an origin [^pasco_2025].
+ Position is defined as the translational distance from a parent frame origin to a child frame origin [^matlab_2025].
+ In geometry, a position or position vector, also known as location vector or radius vector, is a Euclidean vector that represents a point $P$ in space [^wikipedia_2025].
+ At a certain time $t$, the position of a particle may be specified by giving its coordinates $x(t)$, $y(t)$, and $z(t)$ in a particular Cartesian frame of reference [^goodstein_2020].
+ Specifying the position of an object is essential in describing motion [^nave_2017].
+ Position is a vector quantity that specifies the location of a point or object in space with respect to an origin [^gpt-4o_2025].
+ Two quantities, distance and direction, together specify the position [^nerdstudy_2017].
